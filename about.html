About

Description:

ProteinTracker can be used to track your daily protein intake. You can set a daily goal, then choose the food for your meal and for each product add the protein quantity. The Chrome Extension ProteinTracker will notify you when the goal which is set is achieved. This can either make you think about eating less or even, if you want to get a bigger intake of protein, eating more.

Features:

  • The ProteinTracker extension has an options page where you can set the goal and some personal information.
  • You have a popup window where you can type your protein quantity and add it, there the progress bar will show how close to your goal you are in precentage and as a line of progress. This popup was made as embedded as possible to be usefull when tracking the protein intake.
  • You also have the context menu option to add proteins and get closer to the goal, accessible with mouse selection of protein quantity and right click.
